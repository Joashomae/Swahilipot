More of what we do
Social Enterprise Support

We support business that they are primarily set up to achieve a social or environmental mission.

Capacity Building

Swahilipot helps individuals, organizations, and communities to develop the skills, knowledge, and resources they need to effectively achieve their goals and create positive change.

Coworking and Safe Space

We offer spaces independently or for different organizations with a flexible and collaborative work environment, and often include amenities such as high-speed internet, printing and copying facilities, meeting and conference rooms, and shared kitchens and break areas.

Youth Opportunities

These opportunities can take a variety of forms, such as education and training programs, internships and apprenticeships, service learning and volunteer opportunities, and leadership development programs.

Culture of Innovations

Our culture of innovation refers to the values, practices, and behaviors that support and encourage the development and implementation of new and creative ideas within an organization or society.

Mental Wellness

Swahilipot helps members to manage members thoughts, feelings, and behaviors effectively, and to have a sense of purpose and satisfaction in life
